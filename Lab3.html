Lab 3: Glyphs

The data for these maps comes from the 2018 World Happiness Report.

World Happiness Report

Happiness Explanation in Southern Africa in 2018

This map shows some of the ‘explanatory factors’ of happiness in Southern Africa. It effectively conveys happiness explanations, however it is hard to determine the difference in size of the pie charts representing the overall happiness score.

Happiness Scores in Southern Africa in 2018

This map shows several happiness measures in Southern Africa. This map effectively conveys the different GDP between countries, but some of the other factors are hard to view because they have smaller differences between countries.

Happiness Score, Confidence & GDP across Africa in 2018

This map shows attributes from the World Happiness Table 2.1 using size, color and orientation. While this map is displaying several attributes at once, one interesting trend is that more less confident scores seem to have a smaller life ladder score and smaller GDP.

Attributes of Trees in Denver Zoo

This map displays the number of stems per tree and the latitude and longitude. There were not additional numerical or categorical attributes to display, therefore latitude and longitude were selected.
