Blender Lab (Parts 1 & 2)

Figure 1: Dix Park 3-D Model

Figure 1 shows a 3-D rendering of Dix Park in Raleigh, North Carolina. Various shading and texturing parameters were applied by altering the Lighting and Roughness/Glossiness attributes to create the rendering shown above. The interactive 3-D model can be viewed on Sketchfab.

Figure 2: Digital Surface Model: Viewsheds

Figure 2 shows four digitial surface models of Dix Park in Raleigh, North Carolina. The colors and associated annotations symbolize the viewshed, or the view of an area from a specific vantage point as defined by the annotation. Various texture, shading, and filtering parameters were applied to create the rendering shown above. The interactive 3-D model can be viewed on Sketchfab.
