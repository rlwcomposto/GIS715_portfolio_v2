Lab 1: Symbology

Black & White Map

This map displays several features in Pennsylvania with shades of black and white. The design is meant to highlight waterbodies and rivers. These hydrology features pop out in a sharp black from the back drop of other gray features.

A Colorful Map

This map shows the same area as above, with different symbology. The goal of this symbology is to highlight incorporated and unincorporated areas in the Harrisubrg region. The intention is to notice the type of area and then the train lines (thicker and dark purple) and then the roads (thinner and purple).
